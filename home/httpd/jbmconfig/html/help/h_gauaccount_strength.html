Description

For a secure password, choose one that is at least six characters long, which is not a common word and comprised of a mixture of upper and lower case characters and numbers.

  • Minimum 6 characters in length
  • Contains 3/4 of the following items:
    - Uppercase Letters
    - Lowercase Letters
    - Numbers
    - Symbols

Recommended Setting

Strength: Good

Score: Greater than 60
