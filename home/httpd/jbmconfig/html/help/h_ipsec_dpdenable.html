Description

This feature can help detect when a remote end-point is no longer communicating properly. Once an error is detected, the "hold" state will only renegotiate the tunnel after new traffic destined for the tunnel is detected. The "restart" state will attempt to immediately reestablish the connection to the concentrator. For this reason, "restart" may use more bandwidth and may not be the ideal choice for a limited data plan. However, if a host at the central site needs to initiate connections down to a local device through the tunnel, "restart" may be necessary so that the tunnel is always up and waiting for new data from the concentrator.
