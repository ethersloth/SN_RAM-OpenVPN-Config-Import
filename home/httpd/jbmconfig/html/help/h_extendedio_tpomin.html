Description

Min OFF/ON:
Specify a Minimum OFF/ON time (shortest allowable ON or OFF pulse) if your output devices are not capable of changing state within 1 / 32767 of the desired cycle time. (Mechanical relays can be an example of such a device.) The range for the minimum OFF/ON time is 10 mS to 10 minutes. All settings will maintain the time proportion by waiting multiple cycle times, if needed, to turn an output on or off.
The default Min OFF/ON value is 100ms.
